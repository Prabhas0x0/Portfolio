I'm Prabhas, a first-year B.Tech student in Computer Science Engineering. I got into programming because I wanted to understand how things work — and then build my own versions of them.

My focus right now is on foundations: HTML, CSS, Java, and data structures. I think depth matters more than breadth, especially early on. Finishing semester one with a 9.4 CGPA tells me the approach is working.

I care about how software looks and feels, not only whether it runs. That's why my first real project was a music player — something I'd actually want to open every day.
